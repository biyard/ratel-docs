## Spaces

Spaces are structured environments for deliberation and discussion.

### Space Types

**Deliberation Spaces**:
- Pre-survey ‚Üí Discussion ‚Üí Post-survey workflow
- Anonymous or identified participation
- Moderated discussions
- Analytics and reporting

**Team Spaces**:
- Organization-specific areas
- Member-only access
- Collaborative features
- Shared resources

### Space Lifecycle

1. **Created** - Space is being configured
2. **Published** - Open for participants to join
3. **Pre-Survey** - Initial opinions collected
4. **Active Deliberation** - Discussion phase
5. **Final Survey** - Post-deliberation feedback
6. **Completed** - Results available for review

### Joining a Space

When you see an interesting space:

1. **Review Details**:
   - Read the space objectives
   - Check participation requirements
   - View timeline and schedule
   - Understand commitment needed

2. **Accept Invitation**:
   - Click "Join Now"
   - Complete any credential verification
   - Agree to space rules
   - Confirm participation

3. **Complete Pre-Survey**:
   - Answer initial questions
   - Share your baseline opinions
   - Provide demographic info (if required)
   - Submit responses

4. **Enter the Space**:
   - Access discussion boards
   - Read background materials
   - View other participants (if not anonymous)
   - Start engaging

## Discussion Boards

Inside spaces, organized discussion areas:

### Board Structure

**Topics** - Each board focuses on a specific aspect:
- Question or issue statement
- Background information
- Reference materials
- Discussion guidelines

**Posts** - Contributions to the discussion:
- Original posts presenting arguments
- Replies to existing posts
- Threaded conversations
- Multimedia support

**Moderation** - Maintaining quality:
- Creator oversight
- Community flags
- Guideline enforcement
- Civil discourse

### Posting in Discussions

To contribute to a board:

1. **Select a Topic** - Choose the relevant discussion board
2. **Read Existing Posts** - Understand current conversation
3. **Write Your Post**:
   - Clear argument or question
   - Supporting evidence or reasoning
   - Respectful tone
   - Proper citations
4. **Review and Post** - Check for clarity before submitting

### Anonymous Participation

Some spaces allow anonymous posting:

- Your identity is hidden from other participants
- Creator and moderators can see your anonymous ID
- All contributions are tracked anonymously
- Survey responses remain confidential

This encourages honest discussion on sensitive topics.

## Surveys and Polls

Structured data collection within spaces:

### Pre-Survey

Before deliberation begins:
- **Multiple Choice** - Select from predefined options
- **Ranking** - Order preferences
- **Likert Scale** - Rate agreement on statements
- **Open Response** - Free-text answers

Purpose: Capture baseline opinions before discussion influences views.

### Final Survey

After deliberation concludes:
- **Same Questions** - Allows pre/post comparison
- **Opinion Tracking** - Measure how views changed
- **Consensus Analysis** - Identify areas of agreement
- **Reporting** - Data visualization and exports

Purpose: Understand how deliberation affected perspectives.
