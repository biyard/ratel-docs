# Creating Your Account

Welcome! This guide will walk you through creating your Ratel account and getting you ready to participate in deliberations or explore the Fine-Art (d.AGIT) marketplace.

## Before You Start

Make sure you have:
- A valid email address or Google account
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A few minutes to complete the sign-up process

## Sign Up Methods

Ratel offers two convenient ways to create your account:

![Sign In Page](/img/sign-in-initial.png)

### Option 1: Email Sign Up

The traditional email-based registration:

![Email Sign Up Form](/img/email-signup-form.png)

1. **Visit Ratel**
   - Navigate to [https://dev.ratel.foundation](https://dev.ratel.foundation)
   - Click **Sign In** in the top right corner

2. **Start Registration**
   - Click **Create an account** on the sign-in page
   - Enter your email address
   - Click **Send Verification Code**

3. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your inbox for a verification code
   - Enter the 6-digit code in the verification field
   - If you don't see the email, check your spam folder
   - Click **Verify**

4. **Set Your Password**
   - Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm your password by entering it again
   - Click **Continue**

5. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Enter your **display name** (how others will see you)
   - Choose a unique **username** (e.g., @yourname)
   - Usernames must be unique and can contain letters, numbers, and underscores
   - Click **Complete Sign Up**

### Option 2: Google Sign Up

Quick sign-up using your Google account:

You can see the "Continue With Google" button in the sign-in page above.

1. **Visit Ratel**
   - Navigate to [https://dev.ratel.foundation](https://dev.ratel.foundation)
   - Click **Sign In** in the top right corner

2. **Choose Google Sign Up**
   - Click **Continue with Google**
   - You'll be redirected to Google's authentication page

3. **Authorize with Google**
   - Select your Google account
   - Review the permissions Ratel is requesting
   - Click **Allow** to grant access

4. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Your email is automatically verified
   - Enter your **display name** and **username**
   - Click **Complete Sign Up**

## After Account Creation

Once your account is created:

### 1. Welcome Email

You'll receive a welcome email with:
- Confirmation of your registration
- Links to getting started resources
- Tips for your first steps on the platform

### 2. Profile Setup

Consider completing your profile:
- **Profile Picture**: Upload a photo or avatar (optional but recommended)
- **Bio**: Write a brief description about yourself
- **Interests**: Add topics you're interested in
- **Credentials**: Link any verifiable credentials (optional)

See [Profile Settings](../guide/account/profile-settings) for detailed profile customization.

### 3. Explore the Platform

Now you're ready to:
- Browse deliberation spaces in your feed
- Explore the Fine-Art (d.AGIT) marketplace (coming soon)
- Create your first post
- Join community discussions

## Account Security

### Password Best Practices

If you signed up with email:
- Use a unique password you don't use elsewhere
- Minimum 8 characters recommended
- Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Consider using a password manager

### Two-Factor Authentication (Coming Soon)

Enhanced security with 2FA will soon be available to protect your account.

## Troubleshooting

### Email Verification Issues

**Didn't receive verification code?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Ensure you entered the correct email address
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Click "Resend Code" if available

**Verification code expired?**
- Codes are valid for 10 minutes
- Request a new code to continue

### Username Already Taken

If your desired username is unavailable:
- Try adding numbers or underscores (e.g., @yourname_1)
- Use a variation of your name
- Get creative with a unique identifier

### Google Sign Up Issues

**Can't connect with Google?**
- Ensure pop-ups are enabled in your browser
- Check that you're logged into your Google account
- Try using a different browser
- Clear your browser cache and try again
