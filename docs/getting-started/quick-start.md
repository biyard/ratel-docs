# Quick Start Guide

Welcome to Ratel! This guide will help you get started with the platform in just a few minutes.

## What You'll Learn

In this quick start guide, you'll:
- Create your Ratel account
- Understand the main interface
- Join your first deliberation space or explore Fine-Art (d.AGIT)
- Start engaging with the community

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A valid email address or Google account
- Internet connection

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [Ratel](https://dev.ratel.foundation)
2. Click **Sign In** in the top right corner
3. Choose your sign-up method:
   - **Email**: Enter your email and follow the verification steps
   - **Google**: Use your Google account for quick sign-up

For detailed instructions, see [Creating Your Account](./sign-up).

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

After signing up:
1. Set your **display name** - This is how others will see you
2. Choose a unique **username** - Your handle on the platform (e.g., @yourname)
3. Add a **profile picture** (optional but recommended)
4. Write a brief **bio** to introduce yourself

## Step 3: Explore the Platform

### Main Navigation

The Ratel interface is organized into key areas:

**Top Navigation Bar**:
- **Home** - Your personalized feed
- **Membership** - Manage your account and subscriptions
- **Test Report** - View platform testing information
- **Storybook** - Explore UI components
- **Language Selector** - Switch between English and Korean
- **Theme Toggle** - Choose light or dark mode
- **Sign In/Profile** - Access your account

**Sidebar**:
- **Create Post** - Share your thoughts and ideas
- **Hot Promotion** - Featured spaces and discussions

### Your Feed

The home feed shows:
- Recent posts from spaces you've joined
- Deliberation topics open for participation
- Community discussions and updates
- Promoted spaces requiring participants

## Step 4: Choose Your Path

Ratel offers two main experiences:

### For Deliberation & Policy Discussion

If you're interested in structured policy discussions:

1. **Browse Spaces** - Look for deliberation spaces in your feed
2. **Join a Space** - Click on a space card and select "Join Now"
3. **Complete Pre-Survey** - Answer initial questions before deliberation begins
4. **Participate in Discussion** - Share your thoughts on discussion boards
5. **Complete Final Survey** - Provide post-deliberation feedback

See [Creating Spaces](../spaces/creating-spaces) to start your own deliberation.

### For Art Trading & Collection (Fine-Art / d.AGIT)

If you're interested in artwork trading via **d.AGIT** (Digital Assets Guardianship & Interchange Terminal):

1. **Explore Artworks** - Browse available artworks (coming soon)
2. **View Provenance** - Check Electronic Art Records (EAR) for authenticity
3. **Verify Artwork** - Review oracle verification status
4. **Make Purchase** - Complete secure transactions via smart contracts

## Step 5: Engage with the Community

### Creating Posts

1. Click **Create Post** in the sidebar
2. Write your content using the rich text editor
3. Add media, links, or formatting as needed
4. Choose your audience (public or space-specific)
5. Click **Publish**

### Joining Discussions

- **Like** posts that resonate with you
- **Comment** to share your perspective
- **Share** interesting discussions
- **Follow** users whose content you enjoy

## Common Actions

### Receive Invitations

When invited to a deliberation space:
1. Check **My Spaces** for pending invitations
2. Review the space objectives and requirements
3. Accept the invitation to join
4. Complete any required credential verification

### Manage Notifications

Stay updated with platform activity:
- Click the notification icon in the top bar
- Review invitations, mentions, and updates
- Configure notification preferences in settings

## Tips for Success

### For Deliberation Participants

- **Read carefully** - Review all background materials before surveys
- **Be thoughtful** - Take time to consider different perspectives
- **Stay civil** - Maintain respectful dialogue even in disagreement
- **Complete surveys** - Your pre/post responses help measure consensus

### For Content Creators

- **Be clear** - Write concise, well-structured posts
- **Cite sources** - Back up claims with evidence
- **Engage authentically** - Respond to comments and questions
- **Use formatting** - Make posts easy to read with headers and lists

### For Art Market Participants

- **Verify thoroughly** - Check all EAR documentation before purchasing
- **Review oracle status** - Ensure multiple parties have verified authenticity
- **Understand pricing** - Use AI recommendations as guidance, not absolute truth
- **Keep records** - Maintain your own documentation of transactions
