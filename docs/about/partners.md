## Become a Partner

Interested in partnering with Ratel? We'd love to hear from you.

### Partnership Process

**1. Initial Contact**
- Email partnerships@biyard.co
- Describe your organization
- Explain partnership interest
- Share relevant links

**2. Discovery Call**
- Discuss goals and vision
- Explore synergies
- Identify opportunities
- Assess fit

**3. Proposal Development**
- Define partnership scope
- Outline benefits and responsibilities
- Set timelines and milestones
- Agree on terms

**4. Agreement & Launch**
- Formalize partnership
- Execute agreement
- Launch collaboration
- Monitor and optimize

### What to Include

**Organization Overview**
- Mission and vision
- Team and track record
- Current products/services
- User base and reach

**Partnership Proposal**
- Collaboration areas
- Mutual benefits
- Resources committed
- Success metrics

**Timeline & Logistics**
- Proposed start date
- Key milestones
- Contact points
- Communication preferences
