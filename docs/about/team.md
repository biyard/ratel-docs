# Team

Meet the people behind Ratel.

## Core Team

Ratel is built by a diverse team of developers, policy experts, blockchain specialists, and community builders united by a shared vision of participatory governance.

### Development

**Engineering Team**
- Full-stack developers specializing in Rust, TypeScript, and React
- Blockchain developers with Web3 expertise
- DevOps engineers ensuring platform reliability
- Security specialists protecting user data

**Product Team**
- Product managers guiding feature development
- UX/UI designers creating intuitive experiences
- QA engineers maintaining quality standards

### Policy & Governance

Interested in contributing? Here's how:

1. Visit [github.com/biyard/ratel](https://github.com/biyard/ratel)

## Culture

### Remote-First

We believe the best talent is distributed globally. Our team works across time zones, collaborating asynchronously and meeting synchronously when needed.

**Communication Tools:**
- Discord for team chat
- GitHub for code collaboration
- Notion for documentation
- Zoom for video calls

### Transparency

As a platform built on transparency, we practice what we preach:
- Open roadmap
- Public development
- Regular community updates
- Transparent decision-making

### Continuous Learning

The blockchain space evolves rapidly. We invest in continuous learning through:
- Weekly knowledge sharing sessions
- Conference attendance
- Online courses and certifications
- Internal workshops

### Work-Life Balance

Building a sustainable platform requires sustainable practices:
- No crunch culture
- Reasonable work hours
- Encouraged time off
- Mental health support
