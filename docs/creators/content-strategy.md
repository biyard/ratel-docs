# Content Strategy

Develop a content strategy to keep your community engaged.

## Content Planning

### Content Pillars
Define 3-5 core themes:
- Educational content
- Community discussions
- News and updates
- Entertainment/fun
- Member spotlights

### Content Calendar
- Plan content in advance
- Mix content types
- Schedule for peak times
- Leave room for spontaneity

## Content Types

### Educational
- How-to guides
- Explainers
- Tutorials
- Resources

### Discussion
- Open questions
- Polls and surveys
- Deliberation topics
- Debates

### Engagement
- Member highlights
- Challenges and contests
- AMAs (Ask Me Anything)
- Community events

## Content Quality

### What Works
✅ Original insights
✅ Actionable advice
✅ Engaging questions
✅ Visual content
✅ Personal stories

### What Doesn't
❌ Clickbait
❌ Excessive promotion
❌ Controversial for controversy's sake
❌ Recycled content
❌ Inconsistent posting

## Measuring Success

Track metrics:
- Engagement rate (likes, comments)
- Reach and impressions
- Share rate
- Discussion quality
- Member feedback

Adjust strategy based on:
- What resonates most
- Member requests
- Trending topics
- Seasonal changes
