### Email Support
**Email**: [hi@biyard.co](mailto:hi@biyard.co)

**Response Time**:
- General: Within 48 hours
- Account issues: Within 24 hours
- Security: Within 12 hours

**What to Include**:
- Clear description of issue
- Screenshots (if relevant)
- Your username
- Browser and OS
