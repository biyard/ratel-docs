# Account Issues

Solutions to common account problems.

## Can't Sign In

### Reset Password
1. Click "Forgot Password" on sign-in page
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password

### Email Not Received
- Check spam/junk folder
- Add noreply@ratel.foundation to contacts
- Wait 10 minutes and retry

## Account Recovery

### Lost Access to Email
Email [hi@biyard.co](mailto:hi@biyard.co) with:
- Username
- Alternate contact info
- Proof of identity

### Compromised Account
1. Change password immediately
2. Review active sessions
3. Enable 2FA
4. Contact support if needed

## Deleting Account

1. Settings → **Account** → **Delete Account**
2. Confirm deletion
3. 30-day grace period
4. Permanent after 30 days
