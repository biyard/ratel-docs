# Membership Benefits

Benefits of Ratel premium membership.

## Free Tier

All users get:
- Unlimited posts and comments
- Join unlimited spaces
- Basic deliberation participation
- Standard analytics
- Email support

## Premium Tier

Coming soon: $9.99/month

Additional benefits:
- Advanced analytics
- Priority support
- Exclusive badges
- Ad-free experience
- Early feature access
- Increased upload limits
- Custom themes
- Premium spaces access

## Supporter Tier

Coming soon: $29.99/month

All Premium benefits plus:
- Governance voting boost
- Creator revenue share
- Direct team access
- Beta feature testing
- Event priority access
- Custom space features
