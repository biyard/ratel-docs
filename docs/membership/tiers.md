# Membership Tiers

Detailed comparison of Ratel membership tiers.

## Free Tier

**Price**: $0/month

**Features**:
- Unlimited posts
- Join spaces
- Basic deliberation
- 10 images/post
- Community support

## Premium Tier

**Price**: $9.99/month
Coming Q2 2025

**Everything in Free, plus**:
- Advanced analytics
- Ad-free experience
- Priority support
- Exclusive badge
- 50 images/post
- Custom themes
- Early features

**Price**: $29.99/month
Coming Q2 2025

**Everything in Premium, plus**:
- Governance boost
- Revenue sharing
- Direct team access
- Beta testing
- 100 images/post
- API access
- Custom branding
