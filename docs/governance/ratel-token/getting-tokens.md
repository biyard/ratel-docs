# Getting RATEL Tokens

Multiple ways to acquire RATEL tokens.

## Earn Through Participation

### Active Contribution
- Create quality posts
- Engage in deliberations
- Moderate spaces
- Complete surveys
- Verify DID attributes

### Rewards Program
- Daily login bonuses
- Achievement badges
- Referral rewards
- Early adopter bonuses

## Purchase Tokens

Coming Q2 2025:
- Decentralized exchanges (Uniswap, Sushiswap)
- Centralized exchanges (TBA)
- Direct purchase with credit card
- Token presale for early supporters

## Airdrops

Eligibility for airdrops:
- Early platform users
- Active community members
- Governance participants
- Partner communities

Check [Airdrops](/rewards/airdrops/intro) for details.

⚠️ **Important**:

- Verify contract addresses
