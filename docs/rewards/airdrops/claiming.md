# Claiming Your Airdrop

Step-by-step guide to claim RATEL tokens.

## Before You Claim

1. **Wallet Setup**
   - Install MetaMask or compatible wallet
   - Secure your seed phrase
   - Add RATEL token contract (address TBA)

2. **Check Eligibility**
   - Visit ratel.foundation/airdrop
   - Connect wallet
   - View your allocation

## Claiming Process

1. Go to ratel.foundation/airdrop
2. Click "Connect Wallet"
3. Approve connection
4. Review allocation
5. Click "Claim Tokens"
6. Approve transaction
7. Pay gas fee
8. Receive tokens

## Important Notes

- Claim window: 90 days
- Unclaimed tokens return to treasury
- Gas fees paid by claimant
- One claim per address

## After Claiming

- Verify tokens in wallet
- Add to liquidity pool (optional)
- Participate in governance
- Stake for rewards
