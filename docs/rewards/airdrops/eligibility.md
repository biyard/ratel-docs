# Airdrop Eligibility

Criteria for receiving RATEL token airdrops.

## Snapshot Date

TBA - Q1 2025

## Eligibility Criteria

###1. Account Age
- Registered before snapshot
- Verified email
- Active within 30 days

### Tier 2: Active Participation
- Created 10+ posts
- 100+ engagements
- Participated in deliberations
- Completed surveys

### Tier 3: Community Leaders
- Created space
- 100+ space members
- Regular moderation
- High-quality contributions

### Tier 4: Governance
- Participated in Oracle voting
- Verified DID attributes
- Proposed improvements
- Helped others

## Allocation

- Tier 1: 100 tokens
- Tier 2: 500 tokens
- Tier 3: 2,000 tokens
- Tier 4: 5,000 tokens

Cumulative tiers - qualify for multiple!
