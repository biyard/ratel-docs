# Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

## Membership Refunds

### Monthly Subscriptions
- Cancel anytime
- No refunds for partial months
- Access continues until end of billing period

### Annual Subscriptions  
- 30-day money-back guarantee
- Refunds issued to original payment method
- Prorated refunds after 30 days at our discretion

## Premium Features

- One-time purchases: Refundable within 14 days if unused
- In-app credits: Generally non-refundable
- Event tickets: Refundable per event terms

## Web3/Crypto Payments

- Cryptocurrency transactions: Non-refundable due to blockchain immutability
- NFTs and tokens: Subject to smart contract terms
- Gas fees: Non-refundable

## Request a Refund

1. Email [hi@biyard.co](mailto:hi@biyard.co)
2. Include: Order number, reason, payment details
3. Response within 5 business days
4. Refunds processed within 10 business days

## Exceptions

We may issue refunds outside this policy for:
- Technical issues preventing service use
- Duplicate charges
- Unauthorized transactions
