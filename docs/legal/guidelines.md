# Community Guidelines

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

## Core Values

Ratel is built on respectful dialogue, informed participation, and collaborative decision-making.

## Expected Behavior

✅ **Be Respectful**: Engage thoughtfully, even when you disagree
✅ **Be Informed**: Base arguments on facts and cite sources
✅ **Be Inclusive**: Welcome diverse perspectives
✅ **Be Collaborative**: Work together to find common ground

## Prohibited Conduct

❌ Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
❌ Spam or manipulation
❌ Misinformation or fraud
❌ Illegal content or activities
❌ Doxxing or privacy violations

## Deliberation Guidelines

1. Read background materials before participating
2. Complete pre-surveys honestly
3. Engage respectfully with all viewpoints
4. Stay on topic and follow space rules
5. Complete post-surveys to contribute outcomes

## Enforcement

- First violation: Warning
- Second violation: Temporary suspension
- Severe violations: Immediate ban
- Appeals: Contact [hi@biyard.co](mailto:hi@biyard.co)
