# What are Spaces?

Spaces are the core community feature of Ratel, providing collaborative environments for governance, discussion, and decision-making.

## What is a Space?

A Space on Ratel is a dedicated area where communities can:
- Discuss policy proposals and crypto regulations
- Organize collaborative governance activities
- Host deliberations and surveys
- Build focused communities around specific topics

## Types of Spaces

### Deliberation Spaces
Structured environments for formal discussions and policy deliberation.

### Community Spaces
Open spaces for general discussion and community building.

### DAO Spaces
Governance-focused spaces for decentralized organizations.

## Key Features

- **Collaborative Workspaces**: Work together with your community
- **Real-time Updates**: Stay informed with live notifications
- **Team Management**: Organize and manage your Space members
- **Access Control**: Control who can participate in your Space
- **Surveys & Polls**: Gather community feedback
- **Anonymous Participation**: Optional anonymity for sensitive discussions
