# Space Settings

Configure all aspects of your Space.

## General Settings

![Space Settings General](/img/screenshots/spaces/space-settings-general.png)
*Basic Space configuration*

### Basic Information
- **Space Name**: Update display name
- **Description**: Edit space purpose
- **Avatar**: Change space image
- **Banner**: Add cover image
- **Category**: Select relevant category

### Contact & Links
- **Website**: External link
- **Social**: Twitter, Discord, etc.
- **Support Email**: Contact address

## Privacy Settings

![Space Settings Privacy](/img/screenshots/spaces/space-settings-privacy.png)
*Control Space visibility and access*

### Visibility
- **Public**: Discoverable by all
- **Unlisted**: Direct link only
- **Private**: Invitation-only
- **Hidden**: Invisible to non-members

### Join Requirements
- **Open**: Anyone can join
- **Approval**: Manual approval required
- **Invitation**: Invite-only
- **Credential**: Verification required

### Content Privacy
- **Member Content**: Who can see posts
- **Discussion Visibility**: Public or private
- **Analytics**: Share statistics

## Moderation Settings

### Auto-Moderation
- **Spam Filter**: Automatic removal
- **Keyword Filter**: Block specific words
- **Link Filter**: Control external links
- **Media Filter**: Restrict media types

### Reporting
- **Report Threshold**: Auto-action triggers
- **Review Queue**: Manual review
- **Appeal Process**: Dispute resolution

### Member Restrictions
- **Minimum Age**: Account age requirement
- **Karma Threshold**: Reputation needed
- **Rate Limits**: Posting frequency
- **Time Restrictions**: When posts allowed
