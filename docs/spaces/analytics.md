# Analytics

Use analytics to understand and grow your community.

## Available Analytics

### Growth Metrics
- Total members
- New members (daily/weekly/monthly)
- Member retention rate
- Growth trends

### Engagement Metrics
- Posts per day
- Comments per post
- Active members
- Engagement rate

### Content Performance
- Top posts
- Most engaged topics
- Best posting times
- Content type performance

## Accessing Analytics

1. Go to your Space
2. Click **Analytics** tab
3. View dashboards
4. Export data (CSV)

## Key Insights

### What to Track
- Member activity patterns
- Content preferences
- Peak engagement times
- Trending topics

### What to Ignore
- Vanity metrics
- Short-term fluctuations
- Outlier events
- Non-actionable data

## Using Analytics

### Content Strategy
- Post when members are active
- Create more of what works
- Experiment with new formats
- Optimize post length

### Community Growth
- Identify growth sources
- Understand drop-off points
- Improve retention
- Target ideal members

### Deliberation Insights
- Survey participation rates
- Opinion distribution
- Consensus formation
- Discussion quality
