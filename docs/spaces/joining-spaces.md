# Joining a Space

Learn how to discover and join Spaces that interest you.

![Space Discovery](/img/screenshots/spaces/space-discovery.png)
*Discover Spaces that match your interests*

## Finding Spaces

### Explore Spaces
Browse available Spaces through the Explore page.

![Space Card](/img/screenshots/spaces/space-card.png)
*Preview Space details before joining*

### Search for Spaces
Use the search function to find Spaces by topic or keyword.

### Invitation Links
Join private Spaces using invitation links from Space creators.

## Joining Process

### Public Spaces

![Join Space Button](/img/screenshots/spaces/join-space-button.png)
*Click to join a public Space*

1. Navigate to the Space page
2. Click "Join Space"
3. Start participating immediately

![Space Confirmation](/img/screenshots/spaces/space-confirmation.png)
*Welcome message after joining a Space*

### Private Spaces
1. Click the invitation link provided by the Space creator
2. Sign in or sign up if needed
3. Complete any required verification
4. Accept the Space invitation

## Identity Verification

Some Spaces may require identity verification:
1. Enter the authentication code when prompted
2. Complete the verification process
3. Proceed to Space participation

## Pre-Join Surveys

Certain Spaces may ask you to complete a pre-join survey:
1. Review the survey questions
2. Provide your responses
3. Submit the survey
4. Wait for approval if required

## Next Steps

After joining a Space:
- Review the Space overview and guidelines
- Introduce yourself to the community
- Start engaging in discussions
- Participate in governance activities
