## Best Practices

### Value First
✅ Free value to all members
✅ Clear premium benefits
✅ Fair pricing
✅ Transparent monetization

❌ Don't paywall core content
❌ Avoid excessive promotion
❌ Don't ignore free members
❌ No misleading promises

### Sustainability
- Diversify revenue streams
- Plan for long-term
- Reinvest in community
- Balance growth and profit
