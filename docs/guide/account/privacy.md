# Privacy Settings

Control who can see your information and interact with you on Ratel.

![Privacy Settings Overview](/img/screenshots/account/privacy-settings-overview.png)
*Complete overview of all privacy controls in one place*

## Profile Privacy

### Visibility Settings
- **Public Profile**: Visible to everyone
- **Registered Users Only**: Only logged-in users
- **Private Profile**: Only approved followers

### Content Visibility
- **Posts**: Who can see your posts
- **Spaces**: Who can see spaces you're in
- **Activity**: Who can see your likes and comments

## Communication Privacy

### Who Can Contact You
- **Everyone**: Anyone can send messages
- **Connections Only**: Only people you follow
- **No One**: Disable direct messages

### Notifications
- Email notifications for new messages
- Push notifications for mentions
- Weekly digest emails

## Data Privacy

### Data Collection
- **Analytics**: Help improve Ratel (can opt out)
- **Personalization**: Customize your experience
- **Third-party sharing**: Control data shared with partners

### Download Your Data
Export all your Ratel data:
1. Settings → Privacy → **Download Data**
2. We'll email you within 48 hours
3. Download link valid for 7 days

![Data Download Request](/img/screenshots/account/data-download-request.png)
*Request a complete export of your data*

## Blocking and Muting

### Block Users
- They can't see your profile or content
- They can't contact or mention you
- Previous interactions remain visible to you

### Mute Users
- Hide their posts from your feed
- Still see mentions and direct interactions
- They won't know you muted them

## Managing Your Privacy

1. Click profile picture → **Settings**
2. Navigate to **Privacy**
3. Adjust settings as needed
4. Changes take effect immediately
