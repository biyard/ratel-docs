# Profile Settings

Customize your Ratel profile to represent yourself in the community.

## Profile Information

### Basic Information
- **Display Name**: Your public name on Ratel
- **Username**: Your unique @handle
- **Bio**: Brief description (up to 160 characters)
- **Profile Picture**: Square image, recommended 400x400px minimum

![Profile Picture Upload](/img/screenshots/account/profile-picture-upload.png)
*Upload and crop your profile picture with our easy-to-use image editor*

### Extended Profile
- **Location**: City/region (optional)
- **Website**: Personal or professional website
- **Social Links**: Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.

## Editing Your Profile

![Profile Settings Page](/img/screenshots/account/profile-settings-page.png)
*The profile settings page where you can customize all your profile information*

1. Click your profile picture in the top right
2. Select **Settings** → **Profile**
3. Update your information
4. Click **Save Changes**

![Profile Edit Form](/img/screenshots/account/profile-edit-form.png)
*Edit your display name, username, bio, and other profile details*

## Profile Visibility

Control who can see your profile information:

- **Public**: Anyone can see your profile
- **Members Only**: Only registered users
- **Private**: Only people you follow

![Profile Visibility Settings](/img/screenshots/account/profile-visibility-settings.png)
*Choose who can view your profile and posts*

- Use a clear, recognizable profile picture
- Write a concise bio that highlights your interests
- Add relevant social links
