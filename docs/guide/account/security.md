# Account Security

Protect your Ratel account with these security features and best practices.

![Security Settings Page](/img/screenshots/account/security-settings-page.png)
*Comprehensive security controls to protect your account*

## Password Security

### Strong Passwords
- Minimum 8 characters
- Mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
- Avoid common words or patterns
- Don't reuse passwords from other sites

### Changing Your Password
1. Settings → **Security** → **Change Password**
2. Enter current password
3. Enter and confirm new password
4. Click **Update Password**

![Password Change Form](/img/screenshots/account/password-change-form.png)
*Update your password with our secure password change interface*

## Active Sessions

View and manage devices logged into your account:

1. Settings → **Security** → **Active Sessions**
2. See list of active devices and locations
3. Click **Log Out** on unrecognized devices

![Active Sessions](/img/screenshots/account/active-sessions.png)
*Monitor and manage all devices logged into your account*

## Recovery Options

### Email Recovery
- Keep your email address up to date
- Verify your email to enable password reset
- Check spam folder if you don't receive recovery emails
- Use a secure, accessible email account

## Web3 Wallet Security

If you connect a Web3 wallet:

- **Never share** your seed phrase or private keys
- Use hardware wallets for large amounts
- Verify transaction details before signing
- Disconnect wallet when not in use

## Security Alerts

We'll email you when we detect:
- New device login
- Password changes
- Email address changes
- Suspicious activity

## Best Practices

✅ Use a unique, strong password
✅ Keep your email secure
✅ Review active sessions regularly
✅ Don't click suspicious links
✅ Log out from shared devices
✅ Use a password manager

❌ Never share your password
❌ Don't use public WiFi without VPN
❌ Don't reuse passwords
❌ Don't ignore security alerts
❌ Don't save passwords in browsers on shared computers

## Report Security Issues

Suspicious activity? Contact [security@biyard.co](mailto:security@biyard.co)
