# Following Users

Connect with other users and build your network on Ratel.

![Follow Button](/img/screenshots/social/follow-button.png)
*Follow users to see their posts in your feed*

## How to Follow

1. Visit a user's profile
2. Click **Follow** button
3. Their posts appear in your feed
4. You'll see their activity

### Finding Users to Follow

- Browse suggested users
- See who others follow
- Search by username or topic
- Discover through spaces

![Suggested Users](/img/screenshots/social/suggested-users.png)
*Discover users based on your interests*

## Managing Follows

### Your Following List

![Following List](/img/screenshots/social/following-list.png)
*View and manage users you're following*

- View all users you follow
- Sort by: Recently followed, Most active
- Unfollow users
- Organize into lists (coming soon)

### Your Followers

![Followers List](/img/screenshots/social/followers-list.png)
*See who's following you*

- See who follows you
- Follow back with one click
- Block or report followers
- Export follower list

## Follow Features

### Notifications
You'll be notified when:
- Someone you know joins Ratel
- Users you follow post
- Someone follows you back

### Privacy
Control who can follow you:
- **Everyone**: Anyone can follow
- **Approval Required**: Manual approval needed
- **No One**: Disable follows

## Following vs. Connecting

- **Follow**: See their public posts
- **Connect** (coming soon): Mutual relationship
- **Friend** (coming soon): Close connections

## Tips

✅ Follow users with shared interests
✅ Engage with their content
✅ Diversify your network
✅ Review follows periodically

❌ Don't mass follow/unfollow
❌ Avoid following only for follow-back
❌ Don't spam followed users
