# Your Network

Build meaningful connections and grow your network on Ratel.

![Connections List](/img/screenshots/social/connections-list.png)
*View your network of connections*

## Network Features

### Followers
- Users who follow you
- See their profiles
- Follow back easily
- Track follower growth

### Following
- Users you follow
- Organize into groups
- Manage easily
- See mutual connections

### Connections
People who both follow you and you follow:
- Priority in feed
- Enhanced interactions
- Easier messaging
- Collaboration features

## Network Analytics

Coming soon:
- Network growth over time
- Most engaged followers
- Mutual connections
- Network reach

## Network Privacy

Control your network visibility:
- **Public**: Anyone can see
- **Connections Only**: Limited view
- **Private**: Hidden from others

## Growing Your Network

### Authentic Growth
✅ Share quality content
✅ Engage meaningfully
✅ Join relevant spaces
✅ Participate in deliberations

### What to Avoid
❌ Buying followers
❌ Follow/unfollow tactics
❌ Spam following
❌ Fake engagement

## Network Health

Healthy network characteristics:
- Diverse interests
- Active engagement
- Quality over quantity
- Meaningful interactions
