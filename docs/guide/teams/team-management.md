# Team Management

Manage members, settings, and team operations.

![Team Dashboard](/img/screenshots/teams/team-dashboard.png)
*Your team dashboard with overview and quick actions*

## Managing Members

### Adding Members

![Add Members Interface](/img/screenshots/teams/add-members-interface.png)
*Invite new members to your team*

1. Team Settings → **Members**
2. Click **Add Members**
3. Search for users or enter emails
4. Assign roles
5. Send invitations

### Member List

![Member List](/img/screenshots/teams/member-list.png)
*View and manage all team members*

View all members with:
- Profile picture and name
- Role and permissions
- Join date
- Last active
- Quick actions

### Member Actions
- **Change Role**: Promote/demote members
- **Remove Member**: Remove from team
- **Send Message**: Direct message
- **View Profile**: See full profile

### Removing Members

![Remove Member Confirmation](/img/screenshots/teams/remove-member-confirmation.png)
*Confirm before removing a member*

1. Find member in list
2. Click **⋮** menu
3. Select **Remove**
4. Confirm action

Removed members:
- Lose team access immediately
- Can be re-invited later
- Keep individual content
- Removed from team spaces

✅ Regular member reviews
✅ Clear role assignments
✅ Active communication

❌ Don't ignore inactive members
❌ Avoid unclear roles
❌ Don't micromanage
❌ Don't tolerate bad behavior
