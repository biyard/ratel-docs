# Creating Posts

Share your thoughts, questions, and ideas with the Ratel community.

## Create a Post

![Create Post Button](/img/screenshots/posts/create-post-button.png)
*Find the "Create Post" button in your navigation*

1. Click **Create Post** button (top right or sidebar)
2. Write your content
3. Add formatting, media, or polls (optional)
4. Choose audience (public, space-specific, or followers)
5. Click **Post**

![Post Composer](/img/screenshots/posts/post-composer-blank.png)
*The post composer ready for your content*

## Post Types

![Post Type Options](/img/screenshots/posts/post-type-options.png)
*Choose the type of post you want to create*

### Text Posts
- Up to 5,000 characters
- Rich text formatting
- Link previews

### Image Posts
- Upload up to 10 images
- Supported: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
- Max 10MB per image

### Poll Posts
- Add multiple choice questions
- Set poll duration (1 hour to 7 days)
- View real-time results

### Deliberation Posts
- Structured discussion format
- Pre-survey → Discussion → Post-survey
- Available in deliberation spaces

![Audience Selector](/img/screenshots/posts/audience-selector.png)
*Control who can see your post*

## Formatting Options

- **Bold**, *italic*, ~~strikethrough~~
- Headers (H1, H2, H3)
- Bullet and numbered lists
- Block quotes
- Code blocks
- @ mentions
- # hashtags

## Draft Posts

Save drafts to finish later:
1. Write your post
2. Click **Save Draft**
3. Access from Profile → **Drafts**
4. Edit and post when ready

## Best Practices

✅ Write clear, engaging titles
✅ Use paragraphs for readability
✅ Add relevant images
✅ Proofread before posting
✅ Use hashtags for discovery

❌ Avoid all caps (shouting)
❌ Don't post duplicate content
❌ No spam or excessive links
❌ Respect community guidelines
