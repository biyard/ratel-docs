# Threads and Comments

Participate in conversations through comments and threaded discussions.

![Thread View](/img/screenshots/posts/thread-view.png)
*A conversation thread showing replies and nested discussions*

## Commenting on Posts

![Reply Button](/img/screenshots/posts/reply-button.png)
*The reply button on every post*

1. Click on any post to view details
2. Scroll to comment box
3. Write your comment
4. Click **Reply**

![Reply Composer](/img/screenshots/posts/reply-composer.png)
*Reply interface showing connection to parent post*

### Comment Features
- Full text formatting
- @mentions and #hashtags
- Nested replies (up to 5 levels)
- Like and react to comments

## Thread Structure

### Main Post
- Original post starts the thread
- Shows engagement stats (likes, comments, shares)
- Click to expand full thread

### Replies
- Direct replies to main post
- Show under post in chronological order
- Can be sorted by: New, Top, Controversial

### Nested Replies
- Reply to specific comments
- Creates conversation sub-threads
- Maximum 5 levels deep

## Thread Notifications

You'll be notified when:
- Someone replies to your post
- Someone replies to your comment
- Someone mentions you in thread
- Post author replies (if you commented)

### Managing Notifications
- Mute specific threads
- Turn off reply notifications
- Adjust in Settings → Notifications

## Thread Etiquette

✅ Stay on topic
✅ Read full thread before replying
✅ Quote when replying to specific points
✅ Acknowledge good points

❌ Don't derail conversations
❌ Avoid repetitive comments
❌ Don't necropost (reply to very old threads)
